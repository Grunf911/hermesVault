# AlanNotion Promotion Map

## Purpose
A simple map connecting free lead magnets to paid products so promotion can stay focused and reusable.

## Core rule
Use free items to earn attention and trust, then move people toward the most relevant paid product.

## Map

### YouTube lead magnets → YouTube paid offers
Best fits:
- YouTube Thumbnail Master Class → YouTube Operator / YouTube Operator Academy
- YouTube Title Master Class → YouTube Operator / YouTube Operator Academy
- YouTube Video Idea Master Class → YouTube Operator / YouTube Operator Academy
- YouTube Operator Academy - Course Material → YouTube Operator Academy
- YouTube Video Idea Evaluation Checklist → YouTube Operator Academy
- YouTube Title and Thumbnail Ideation Checklist → YouTube Operator Academy
- YouTuber's Tag Manager → YouTube Operator / YouTube Operator Academy

Why:
- These leads already attract the exact audience who cares about YouTube systems, packaging, and execution.
- They naturally build belief in the broader YouTube operator ecosystem.
- YouTube Operator is the direct paid workspace for creators who want the full production pipeline, while YouTube Operator Academy remains the broader learning layer.

### Productivity lead magnets → Productivity paid offers
Best fits:
- Simple Projects & Tasks Tracker → Goals Projects & Tasks
- Ultimate Coffee & Habit Tracker → Goals Projects & Tasks
- Simple CV → Job Seeker Dashboard
- Job Seeker - Application Tracker → Job Seeker Dashboard
- Sales Tracker → Automated Sales OS

Why:
- These free tools show the quality of the system.
- They make the paid version feel like the obvious upgrade.

## Secondary uses
Some lead magnets can also support the broader AlanNotion brand:
- YouTube freebies can support YouTube Operator Academy marketing
- Productivity freebies can support trust-building on YouTube and X
- Sales Tracker can support both sales-system content and creator-ops content

## Recommended promotional ladders

### Ladder 1: YouTube operator funnel
Free YouTube checklist or master class
→ YouTube tutorial content
→ YouTube Operator / Academy

### Ladder 2: Productivity funnel
Free tracker or CV template
→ short demo content
→ paid tracker or dashboard

### Ladder 3: Sales system funnel
Free Sales Tracker
→ automation / workflow content
→ Automated Sales OS

## Weekly operating loop
1. Pick one paid product to push this week
2. Choose the matching free lead magnet
3. Create one YouTube post/video idea around the pain point
4. Create one X post/thread based on the same angle
5. Point people to the free lead magnet first, then the paid product
6. Review what got clicks and saves

## Practical rule
If a lead magnet does not clearly support a paid product, keep it as general trust-building content and do not force a hard sell.
