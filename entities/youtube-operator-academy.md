# YouTube Operator Academy

## Overview
YouTube Operator Academy is a paid Skool community that helps creators grow on YouTube without burnout.

## Positioning
- Offer: structured execution environment with feedback
- Platform: Skool
- Audience: creators who have already published about 10–20 videos
- Promise: trajectory, clarity, and proof of progress without chasing virality
- Business role: education / community business

## Core beliefs
- Creators fail because they focus on the wrong things, in the wrong order, with no feedback loop
- Clarity, focus, and systems beat talent and randomness
- The community is a conversion and belief environment, not just a discussion space

## Product philosophy
- Teach principles, judgment, and decision-making
- Reduce cognitive load and overwhelm
- Encourage action over theory
- Keep accountability medium, blunt, and respectful
- Do not normalize guru tactics, hacks, or burnout
