# Theo Journal — Goals, Projects & Tasks Dashboard

Date: 2026-04-28
Agent: Theo
Timezone: Europe/Stockholm

## What we discussed
- The Goals, Projects & Tasks Notion dashboard and its three underlying databases.
- How Goals, Projects, and Tasks relate to each other.
- The hidden SetFocus behavior on Goals and the focus filters on Projects and Tasks.

## What I learned
- GoalsIntDB is the long-term layer.
- ProjectsDB is the mid-term container layer.
- TasksDB is the execution layer.
- SetFocus on a Goal acts like focus mode: only items directly tied to the focused Goal show up in focused Projects/Tasks views.
- This lets you brain-dump freely without cluttering the active work lane.

## Mistakes I made or wrong assumptions
- None significant; this was mostly model-building and confirmation.

## Recommended corrections to avoid repeating them
- Use Goal for direction, Project for multi-step initiatives, Task for execution.
- When focus mode is on, keep unrelated backlog items out of the current lane and let the filters do the work.

## Final takeaway
- The dashboard is built to keep long-term planning, mid-term initiatives, and daily execution separate while still allowing quick capture.
