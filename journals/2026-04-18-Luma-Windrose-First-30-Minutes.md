## Output produced

### Video angle
A beginner-friendly YouTube video focused on the first 30 minutes of Windrose, framed as a practical survival and onboarding guide.

### Working title ideas
1. Windrose: First 30 Minutes
2. Windrose Beginner Guide: What to Do First
3. Windrose Starter Guide — Don’t Make These Mistakes
4. Windrose: 10 Things I Wish I Knew Earlier
5. Windrose Early Game Tips That Save You Hours

### Thumbnail concept
A simple early-game survival thumbnail with:
- a tense character or ship/player pose
- a visible danger cue or resource cue
- bold text implying urgency, like “FIRST 30 MIN” or “START HERE”

Goal: make the viewer feel this is the shortest path to avoiding early mistakes and getting oriented fast.

### Hook options
1. If you start Windrose the wrong way, you waste your first 30 minutes fast — here’s the cleanest opening route.
2. Windrose looks simple at first, but the early game punishes bad habits — these are the things to do immediately.
3. In the first 30 minutes of Windrose, there are a few decisions that make the whole game easier.
4. I’m going to show you exactly what to do first in Windrose so you don’t lose time, gear, or progress.
5. If you just installed Windrose, this is the fastest way to avoid the common early-game traps.

### Script outline
1. Open with the core promise: show viewers what to do first so the early game goes smoothly.
2. Explain the main early-game priorities.
3. Cover the first important controls and survival habits.
4. Show the first useful upgrades or unlocks to aim for.
5. Explain what not to waste time on early.
6. End with a clear “next step” so the viewer knows what to do after the first 30 minutes.

### Windrose starter tips captured for the video
- Rebind target lock early
- Don’t hold block forever
- Learn parry timing
- Prioritize inventory space
- Upgrade carry space
- Grab early points of interest
- Keep food buffs active
- Keep backup food at base
- Use the ship surgeon
- Build comfort early

### Notes on alignment
The title, thumbnail, and hook all point to the same promise: help the viewer survive and orient themselves in Windrose’s early game without wasting time.

### Final takeaway
This proposal is meant to be a practical first-session guide for Windrose, centered on early survival, movement, and avoiding beginner mistakes.
